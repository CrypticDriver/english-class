📱 话题 ① 如果设备会说话

Describe what your devices would say about you if they could talk. — 适合 Part 2，展示个性与幽默

📝 Part 2 完整范文

If my devices could suddenly talk, I think I'd be in a lot of trouble — honestly, they'd probably gang up on me.

Let's start with my phone, because it's the one I'm closest to — maybe too close. It would definitely call me out for being glued to the screen. I check it the moment I wake up, and it's the last thing I see before bed. It would probably say something like, "Do you even remember life before me?" And the embarrassing part is, it knows all my secrets — my random 2 a.m. searches, the videos I'd never admit to watching.

Then there's my smartwatch, which would basically be the health-obsessed friend in the group. It's always nagging me about standing up, drinking water, getting more sleep. If it could talk, it would sigh and say, "I told you to go to bed at eleven, and here you are scrolling at one."

And finally, my fridge — that one would just roast me. It would tease me for buying fresh vegetables with good intentions and then letting them go bad while I order takeout instead.

But joking aside, I think there's something a little unsettling about it. These devices know me better than some of my closest friends do. They see the version of me that nobody else does — the late-night snacker, the procrastinator, the person who talks big but doesn't always follow through.

So if they could talk, I think they'd have a lot to say — and most of it would be painfully accurate.

🔑 词组精讲（按出现顺序）

词组中文意思用法 / 为什么好用
be in a lot of trouble麻烦大了开场制造幽默，口语很自然
gang up on sb.联合起来对付某人形象，几个设备"组团吐槽你"

💡 为什么能拿高分

  • 拟人化贯穿全篇 — 三个设备三种性格（损友 / 健康狂 / 毒舌厨房），有画面感
  • 幽默 + 深度 — 前半搞笑，joking aside 一转进入"设备比朋友更懂我"的反思
  • 自然口语标记 — honestly / the embarrassing part is / who am I kidding 让你像母语者，不是背书

🤝 话题 ② 更连接还是更孤独

Are people more connected or more lonely than 10 years ago? — 偏 Part 3 思辨，句式更"论证型"

🎯 核心观点（两面都谈，再选一边）

➕ "更连接"这一面
  • 随时能联系到任何人，跨国视频通话像面对面
  • 找到同好社群更容易（小众爱好也有圈子）
➖ "更孤独"这一面（通常更有说服力）
  • "connected but alone" — 点赞代替真实陪伴，群里 500 人却没人能深夜倾诉
  • 面对面社交能力退化，宁可发消息也不愿打电话
  • 社交媒体制造"别人都过得更好"的焦虑感

🗣️ 实用句式

句式用法说明
On the surface, we're more connected than ever, but deep down, many people feel lonelier.经典"表面 vs 深层"对比开场
We have hundreds of online friends, yet few people we can truly count on.count on = 依靠，反差有力
Social media gives us the illusion of connection.illusion of… 高分抽象名词结构
Ten years ago, people actually called each other; now we just send texts.今昔对比，actually 加强语气
Everyone online seems to be living their best life.地道俚语，讲社媒焦虑必备
Technology has made us physically connected but emotionally distant.对仗金句，直接定调
